# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want SmartSRE Copilot to be a respectful, practical, and welcoming project
for maintainers, contributors, users, and AI coding agents working alongside
humans.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, direct, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still verifying technical claims.
- Keep discussions focused on the work and its impact.
- Credit others' ideas and contributions.
- Prefer actionable feedback over vague criticism.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass security, review, or quality gates.
- Submitting or encouraging secret leakage.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, close issues, block users, or
pause participation when behavior harms the project or its community.

Security-related concerns should follow `SECURITY.md`.
